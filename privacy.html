Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 6, 2026

Who we are

Bryant Agent Workspace ("Bryant," "we," "us") is a personal AI assistant platform operated by Darryl Bryant, an independent professional based in the United States. Contact: darryl.bryant23@gmail.com.

What this policy covers

This policy explains what information we collect from operators and authorized clients of the Bryant Agent Workspace platform (the "Service"), how we use it, and the rights you have over it.

Information we collect

Information you provide directly

  • Name, business name, email address, and phone number (provided when an operator onboards a client)
  • Commands, briefs, and content you submit to the AI assistants
  • Communications you send to or receive from the platform via SMS or web

Information collected automatically

  • Timestamps and metadata of commands and approvals
  • Limited technical data needed to operate the service (session identifiers, request logs)

Information from third parties

  • Limited technical data from integrated services (Twilio for SMS delivery, OpenAI for AI processing, Google for Gmail/Calendar/Docs when explicitly connected by the operator)

How we use information

  • To deliver requested AI-generated content (drafts, briefs, calendars, reminders)
  • To deliver and confirm SMS messages between authorized clients and the operator
  • To maintain the approval flow that protects clients from unintended actions
  • To prevent abuse, enforce access controls, and secure the platform

We do not sell, rent, or share personal information with advertisers. We do not use your data to train AI models. We do not contact unauthorized recipients.

SMS messaging specifically

Phone numbers collected for SMS use are used solely for the SMS messaging service described at registration. Mobile information will not be shared with or sold to third parties for marketing or promotional purposes. Information sharing is limited to the technical providers required to deliver the message (currently Twilio).

Data retention

We retain command logs, approvals, and message history for as long as the operator maintains the platform. Operators may delete records at any time from the dashboard. Phone numbers and PINs are removed when an operator revokes a client's access.

Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your data at any time by contacting darryl.bryant23@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

Children

The platform is not intended for use by individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

Security

The platform uses industry-standard transport encryption (HTTPS) and stores data in a secured backend. API keys provided by operators are stored server-side and are never exposed to the browser.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect the most recent change.
